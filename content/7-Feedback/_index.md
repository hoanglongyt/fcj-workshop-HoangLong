### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
Each week, the program focuses on a new set of knowledge, helping me access a lot of useful information for future projects. Concepts like IAM, networking, or monitoring seemed quite disconnected when read individually, but when deploying a whole system together, I understood how they work with each other.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
During the process of completing the report, there were times when I was unsure which content was appropriate to present and what evidence was sufficient to include in the report. I proactively discussed with my mentor and the admin team, cross-referenced with the guidelines, and verified practical results through CLI output, screenshots, and actual deployment results. Thanks to this, the report content accurately reflects the implementation process, ensuring accuracy and staying close to the work I directly performed.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The work assigned to me aligns well with the knowledge I learned at university, while also expanding into new areas that I had never been exposed to before. Thanks to this, I both reinforced my foundational knowledge and gained additional practical skills.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I learned many new skills such as using project management tools, teamwork skills, and professional communication in a corporate environment. The mentor also shared valuable real-world experiences that helped me better plan my career path.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit**  
The working culture is respectful, equal, and oriented toward common goals. Everyone works seriously, yet the atmosphere is always comfortable and pleasant. Even as an intern, I always felt that my opinions were respected like an official team member.
